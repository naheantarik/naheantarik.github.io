QuiZCuBes is a web application, which will help teachers and students. The administration will have full control over the applicationâ€™s performance. Teachers can create courses and create quizzes based on their subjects. Teachers will give a code to the student for enrolling in the curse. By the code, students can enroll in the course. Teachers and students can edit their information. A teacher has control over theircourse. The teacher will create questions and also fix the time of a quiz. There can be three types of questions- MCQ, true/false, and theory-based. When the time will over, the answer will be auto-submitted. When the student attends the quiz, they will get attendance for that class. After the exam, there will be a discussion for questions and answers. Students will discuss their answers and can also share images. Students will get points according to the results. Students will be awarded for good results. Students can see their progression on the leaderboard. Based on the result this system will give feedback about students.

Nowadays, it is very difficult to handle a lot of papers, questions, and store students result. Teachers have to check the exam manually. It takes a lot of times. Sometimes the test copy is lost. Students must wait for their results until the teacher has completed script checking. QuiZCuBes was developed to overcome the flaws in the traditional practice manual system for managing question, result. It gives facility to teacher and student. From anywhere, it allows a student to participate in a quiz. It keeps track of all the information about the student, quiz, and result
